Easy Chili

This quick and easy meal is great for any night of the week. Server over rice and garnish with sour cream and cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can of tomato soup
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • optional: hot sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Add all other ingredients to a sauce pan and simmer over medium heat.
  3. Combine beef when fully cooked.
  4. Enjoy!
