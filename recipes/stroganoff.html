Hamburg Stroganoff

Another easy recipe that is a household staple!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small can of mushroomm pieces
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 2 cups mayo
  • 4 cloves of fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 bag of egg noodles
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add diced onion, garlic(crushed), rosemary, and mushroom to a pan on medium heat with some olive oil. Cook until onions become translucent.
  2. Add beef to pan. Cook until browned.
  3. Add sour cream and may to pan and mix well. Let simmer while you cook the noodles.
  4. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook noodles as directed by packaging.
  5. Serve separate.
