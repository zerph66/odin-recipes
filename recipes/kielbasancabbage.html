Kielbasa and Cabbage

Another easy and cheap recipe that is a household staple!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of kielbasa
  • 1 bag of coleslaw
  • 1 small onion
  • 4 cloves of fresh garlic
  • 1 pack of your favorite noodles
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add diced onion, kielbasa (sliced), and garlic (crushed) to a pan on medium heat with some olive oil. Cook until onions become translucent.
  2. Add cabbage to pan and cover. Cook until cabbage is soft.
  3. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook noodles as directed by packaging.
  4. Mix and serve.
